About TechFix IT Solutions

Our Story

The Johannesburg-based company TechFix IT Solutions was established in 2015 and specializes in providing IT consulting services to individuals and small businesses, as well as hardware repairs and software debugging.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide quick, dependable, and reasonably priced IT help.

Our Vision
