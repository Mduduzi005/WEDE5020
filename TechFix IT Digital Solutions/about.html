Our Story

Founded in 2015 in Johannesburg, TechFix IT Solutions was created with the vision of helping individuals, students, and small businesses overcome everyday technology challenges. What started as a small repair service quickly grew into a trusted IT solutions provider offering consulting, hardware, and software support.

Our Mission

To provide quick, dependable, and affordable IT help to students, home users, and small businesses — ensuring that technology empowers rather than frustrates.

Our Vision

To become South Africa’s go-to source for IT services for small businesses, known for innovation, reliability, and customer-first service.

Our Core Values

  • Customer Focus: Every solution is tailored to the client’s needs.
  • Transparency: Clear communication and no hidden fees.
  • Innovation: Keeping up with the latest tech trends.
  • Reliability: Fast turnaround times and trustworthy service.
  • Community: Supporting students and small businesses in Johannesburg.

Who We Serve

  • Students in need of dependable IT support
  • Home office professionals
  • Small business owners
  • Startups requiring technical infrastructure
  • Educational institutions needing IT maintenance
